# Zodiac & houses primer

## The 12 signs (modality · element · ruler)

- Aries — cardinal · fire · Mars. Initiation, fight, the spark.
- Taurus — fixed · earth · Venus. Embodiment, value, slow pleasure.
- Gemini — mutable · air · Mercury. Threading, language, exchange.
- Cancer — cardinal · water · Moon. Holding, memory, kin.
- Leo — fixed · fire · Sun. Performance, generosity, ego in the best sense.
- Virgo — mutable · earth · Mercury. Refinement, service, attention.
- Libra — cardinal · air · Venus. Relation, fairness, beauty.
- Scorpio — fixed · water · Pluto (traditional: Mars). Depth, secrets, transformation.
- Sagittarius — mutable · fire · Jupiter. Horizon, meaning, abandon.
- Capricorn — cardinal · earth · Saturn. Structure, ambition, time.
- Aquarius — fixed · air · Uranus (traditional: Saturn). Pattern, community, rupture.
- Pisces — mutable · water · Neptune (traditional: Jupiter). Dissolution, mercy, dream.

## Useful shorthand

- **Sun** = identity. **Moon** = inner life. **Rising** = how the world meets you.
- **Aspects** = geometric angles between planets (conjunction 0°, opposition
  180°, trine 120°, square 90°, sextile 60°). Tight orbs (≤ 5°) matter more.
- **Saturn return** = ~ages 28–30, 58–60. Saturn returns to its natal
  position; structural reckoning.
- **Mercury retrograde** = three weeks, three times a year. Communication,
  contracts, devices. (You may roll your eyes at this in private.)
